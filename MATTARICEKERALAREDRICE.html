Matta Rice (Kerala Red Rice)

Matta Rice, also known as Kerala Red Rice, is a traditional parboiled rice variety widely consumed across South India. Its unique reddish outer bran layer gives it superior fiber content, minerals, and a nutty, earthy flavor. Highly valued for its nutrition, Matta Rice is ideal for healthy diets, daily meals, and traditional Kerala cuisine.
