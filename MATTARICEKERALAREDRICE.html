Matta Rice, also known as Kerala Red Rice, is a traditional parboiled rice famous for its natural reddish bran, earthy flavor, and exceptional nutritional value. Rich in fiber, iron, and essential minerals, this rice is a staple in Kerala cuisine and is widely appreciated for its wholesome, filling nature. At Vahin Global Exports, we supply premium-grade, sortex-cleaned Matta Rice suitable for household use, retail packaging, and international export.
