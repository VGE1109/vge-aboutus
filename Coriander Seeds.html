Coriander Seeds

Coriander Seeds (Coriandrum sativum) are among the oldest spices used across world cuisines. Known for their citrusy fragrance and warm flavor, they are essential in spice blends, seasonings, herbal remedies, and food processing industries. Vahin Global Exports supplies premium Indian coriander sourced from leading spice belts including Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh.
