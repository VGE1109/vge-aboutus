Byadgi Red Chilli – Famous for Natural Color & Global Aroma

Byadgi Red Chilli is one of India’s most renowned chilli varieties, widely celebrated for its deep natural red color, mild heat, and distinctively wrinkled appearance. Grown mainly in Karnataka’s Byadgi region, this chilli has gained strong international demand for its high capsanthin content and aromatic flavor profile.

Unlike extremely spicy chilli varieties, Byadgi offers a balanced pungency while delivering outstanding color value—making it a preferred choice for food processors, spice manufacturers, oleoresin extraction units, and cosmetic industries. The chillies are traditionally sun-dried, ensuring better retention of essential oils, natural pigments, and shelf life.
