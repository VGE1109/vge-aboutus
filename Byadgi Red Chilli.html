Byadgi Red Chilli

Byadgi Red Chilli is renowned for its intensely rich red colour, naturally wrinkled texture and mild pungency. Sourced from the Byadgi belt of Karnataka, it is globally valued for its high capsanthin content — the natural pigment responsible for its strong colouring ability.

Quality & Processing

Vahin Global Exports works directly with experienced growers and certified processing units to supply clean, well-graded, low-moisture Byadgi chillies.
