Cloves are one of the world’s most treasured spices, known for their warm aroma, sharp flavor, and strong natural oils. At Vahin Global Exports, we source superior-grade Indian cloves from Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Karnataka—regions renowned for producing deep-colored buds with high eugenol content.
