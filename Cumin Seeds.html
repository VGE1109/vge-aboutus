Cumin Seeds

Cumin Seeds (Cuminum cyminum), popularly known as Jeera, are one of the most valued spices in global cuisine. Their bold, earthy flavor and distinctive aroma make them essential in spice blends, food processing, herbal remedies, and traditional wellness systems. Vahin Global Exports delivers premium-quality cumin seeds sourced from India’s leading spice-growing regions, processed with strict hygiene and advanced cleaning systems.
