S4 — Sannam Red

S4 Sannam Red Chilli, also called Guntur Sannam, is one of India’s most recognized chilli varieties. Known for its rich red color, balanced pungency and sharp aroma, it is widely used in spice processing, powders, blends and ready-to-eat foods.
