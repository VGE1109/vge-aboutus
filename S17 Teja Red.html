S17 — Teja Red

S17 Teja Red is a highly valued Indian chilli variety known for its intense heat, vivid red hue, and natural color integrity. Grown mainly in Guntur (Andhra Pradesh), these long, thin, sun‑dried chillies are prized both for culinary use and industrial extraction.

Quality & Processing

Handpicked at full maturity, our Teja chillies are naturally dried and carefully sorted to remove foreign material. We partner directly with experienced farmers and GMP‑certified processors to guarantee consistent batches — ideal for food processors, spice blenders, and capsaicin extraction units.
