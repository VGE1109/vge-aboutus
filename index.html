About Us

Vahin Global Exports, founded in 2023 by Mr. Vamshi Nath, is a professionally managed Indian export company driven by a clear vision to deliver high-quality, authentic Indian products to global markets with consistency, transparency, and reliability. Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, the company operates as a modern export house, combining strong domestic sourcing capabilities with a deep understanding of international trade requirements. What began as a focused initiative exporting handcrafted goods and festival event items to the USA, UK, and Australia has today evolved into a trusted multi-category export enterprise serving buyers across multiple regions.

Our Journey

The journey of Vahin Global Exports began with building early international relationships and gaining hands-on exposure to global quality standards and buyer expectations. In March 2023, the company successfully completed its first registered overseas shipment, starting with fresh fruits and vegetables. As global demand increased, Vahin Global Exports strategically expanded its portfolio, with a strong and growing emphasis on premium Turmeric Fingers and Turmeric Powder, positioning them as its core export offerings. Alongside turmeric, the company supplies rice varieties such as Sona Masoori, Basmati, Ponni, and Idli/Samba rice, as well as pulses, lentils, whole and ground spices, blended masalas, edible oils, ghee, pickles, snacks, ready mixes, and other Indian grocery and FMCG products.

In addition to food and agricultural commodities, Vahin Global Exports exports electrical equipment including ceiling fans, table and pedestal fans, and air coolers, along with household and personal care products, utensils, cookware, and cultural essentials. Supported by a strong sourcing network across India, the company caters to importers, wholesalers, distributors, retail chains, and institutional buyers across Africa, the Middle East, Europe, the UK, North America, and Australia. Offering both bulk and retail-ready shipments, custom packaging, private labeling, export documentation, and end-to-end logistics support, Vahin Global Exports continues to strengthen its position as a reliable global sourcing partner committed to long-term relationships, competitive pricing, and international quality standards.
