About Vahin Global Exports – Trusted Indian Export Company

Vahin Global Exports is a professionally managed export company based in Hyderabad, India, specializing in the global supply of premium-quality Indian rice, turmeric, spices, pulses, FMCG products, electrical equipment, and household goods.

Established in 2023, the company operates as a reliable sourcing and export partner for importers, wholesalers, distributors, supermarket chains, and institutional buyers worldwide.

Our Core Export Categories

  • Rice Exporter: Sona Masoori, Basmati, Ponni, Idli/Samba Rice
  • Spices & Turmeric: Turmeric Fingers, Turmeric Powder, Whole & Ground Spices
  • Pulses & Lentils
  • Edible Oils, Ghee, Pickles & Ready Mixes
  • Electrical Equipment Export: Ceiling Fans, Table Fans, Pedestal Fans, Air Coolers
  • FMCG Products & Groceries
  • Household & Kitchen Essentials
  • Private Label & Custom Packaging Solutions
