About Vahin Global Exports

Vahin Global Exports is an experienced exporter of Indian rice varieties — supplying bulk commodities to wholesalers and customised retail packaging for supermarkets, specialty stores, and private-label brands. We handle procurement, quality checks, custom milling, private labelling and international logistics to deliver consistent, export-grade rice worldwide.

Our export capabilities: FCL/LCL shipments, container stuffing, fumigation & phytosanitary documentation, buyer / supplier inspections, and flexible MOQ for bulk & retail orders.
